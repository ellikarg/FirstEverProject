Alessio Mida

Photographer, Filmmaker, Storyteller

Born in Rome the same year CD was invented, Argentina took back Malvinas Islands, Italy won its 3rd world cup, I start very early to be interested in movies, agriculture, history and football.
Background in foreign languages, arts and sport, I worked now for 14 years as a photographer and videographer, and willing to undertake a new chapter of my life, more focused in journalism, reportage, social issues.
